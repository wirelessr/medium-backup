I am wondering what kind of integration is.

In this e-commerce example, we adopt the choreography pattern to proceed a whole transaction step by step. Nevertheless, if we want to serve a one-time request, we still can leverage orchestration pattern to retrieve all info at once.

The proper approach depends on how often this “integration” occurs, how much data is available, and what level of consistency is required, etc.
